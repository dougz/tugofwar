In this game of clowns playing tug-of-war, each pair of clowns is fighting over a related pair of words, one of which has a positive connotation, the other a negative. The words have been disguised though, by having one letter added to each, and anagramming the result. Picking the correct word will advance you to the next pair; the wrong word will ruin your progress. The extra letters that were added to the positive word in each pair will spell an answer phrase - THE CLOWNS MARCH ON BUT WHO WROTE MIDDLEMARCH, and the answer is GEORGE ELIOT.
